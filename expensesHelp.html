Expenses Help

On this page, you can add your expense categories with the corresponding amounts. You can also update existing expenses. A pie chart will display the distrubution of your expenses.

  • Add Categories: To add a category to your expenses, click on the "Add Category" button. Then enter the name and amount of your expense. After clicking "Save", the expense will be displayed on the pie chart.
  • Update Category: To update a category, click on a category on the pie chart. The category will be outlined once you click it. After selecting the catgory, click on the "Update" button. A pop-up will be displayed, showing the information relating to the category. Make any necessary changes and click "Save". The updates should be reflected in the pie chart.
  • Delete a Category: To delete a category, click on a category on the pie chart and then click the "Update" button. A pop-up will be displayed, showing the information relating to the category. Click on the trash can in the upper right hand corner and the category will be deleted from the pie chart.
