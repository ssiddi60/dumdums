Wishlist Help

The Wishlist allows you to add, update, and delete the items that you would like to save up for. As you add to your savings, the wishlist will update to show your progess towards each goal.

  • Add to Wishlist: To add to the wishlist, click on the "Add Wish" button. Then enter the name and price of the item. After clicking on "Save", your wish should be added to the list.
  • Update Wishlist: To update the wishlist, click on the pencil icon of the item you would like to update. Make any necessary changes to the item and click "Save". The item should reflect the updates made.
  • Delete from Wishlist: To delete from the wishlist, click on the pencil icon of the item you would like to delete. Then, click on the trash can icon in the upper right hand corner. The item should be removed from the wishlist.
