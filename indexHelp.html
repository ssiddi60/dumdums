Welcome to E$$entially!

E$$entially is a web application that allows you to easily keep track of your expenses, savings, and wishlist. Happy budgeting!

  • Expenses: On this page, you can add your expense categories with the corresponding amounts. You can also update existing expenses. A pie chart will display the distrubution of your expenses.
  • Savings: The Savings tab allows you to add or subtract from the current total. The current total is always diplayed so you know exactly how much money is allocated in savings.
  • Wishlist: The Wishlist allows you to add, update, and delete the items that you would like to save up for. As you add to your savings, the wishlist will update to show your progess towards each goal.
